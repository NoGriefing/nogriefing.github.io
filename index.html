About I •
No Griefing 2020-2025
Macbook Mini (2014)
No Griefing is a Minecraft server I run in my closet on an old 2014 Mac mini. I started it originally as a quarantine project to keep in touch with friends during the worldwide lockdown of 2020. It's been through countless worlds and versions, but right now it's running Better Than Adventure, a mod for Minecraft Beta 1.7.3. It heavily expands the game's content while keeping the core design philosophy alive: a game with no goal- no winners or losers. It's a really cool spot to hang out and meet new friends, so if you have time, feel free to check it out! Everyone is welcome!
